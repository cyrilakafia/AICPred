ACICpred

ACICPred is a web application for predicting anti-inflammatory molecules capable of ameliorating the cytokine storm by inhibiting the toll-like receptor 4 (TLR4) The machine learning models were trained on 196,655 TLR4 inhibitors and non-inhibitors XGBoost (XGB) which was the top performing model obtained a precision of 90%.
